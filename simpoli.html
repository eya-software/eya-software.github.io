Why Simpoli?

We make news and policies accessible for everyone in a unique way. We primarily focus on 4 core values to interest readers.

Simple

Our application breaks down complex political topics and explains them in a way every reader can understand regardless of political experience.

Unbiased

Our articles are driven by presenting facts in an understandable format instead of presenting opinions and partisan analysis.

Engaging

The team at Simpoli has spent countless hours developing an engaging and fun user interface and experience to make news fun again.

Curated

Instead of overwhelming you with constant news articles on every issue in our government, our team spends time finding the most impactful story everyday and the most important policy of the week to deliver relevant information.
